Centurion University of Technology and Management, Odisha

Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM) is the first private University in Odisha which was established through the CUTM Act 4 of the Odisha State Legislative Assembly in 2010. In due course, it has got recognition as Grade-A+ University by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Ministry of HRD and 12 B status by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Centurion University’s School of Vocational Education and Training has been recognized as a Centre of Excellence by Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India and MSS School of Agriculture & School of Fisheries have been accredited by ICAR, India. This University is duly recognized as a pioneer in ‘Skill Integrated Higher Education”. Centurion has embraced the Agenda for Sustainable Development and the associated Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) since being formally announced in 2015. Whilst having an indirect impact and contribution on almost all 17 SDGs, Centurion has specifically focused on 9 SDGs and embedded it in each vertical from its strategy, governance, institutional management and outcomes.
Its unique model lays specific emphasis on creating sustainable livelihoods on a national scale in challenging geographies through education that results in employability and sparks entrepreneurship. This model has been recognized by multiple Governments (Central and State), International Organizations such as UNESCO and the World Bank as well as Policy Think-tanks such as the Niti Ayog. It is the only University in India to be recognized as such. Centurion has focused on ‘hands-on’, ‘experience based’, ‘practice oriented’ learning that creates transformative impact through community centric innovation and action research. While promoting Nano, Mini and Micro Enterprises, the University works toward learning experiences that are ‘quantifiable’, ‘sustainable’, ‘scalable’ and ‘replicable’. Centurion University has both built and participated in building eco-systems that include and integrate the community around it’s campuses and beyond, industry, entrepreneurs and other education institutions. Its education and training delivery approach is:
• Skill Integrated and Employability Linked Teaching/Learning Process
• Focuses/Leverages Industry and Community Partnerships
• Creates/co-creates Enterprises

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering understands the need of ever-changing technology that industry looks for trained professionals to work in IT and IT enabled service industry and sectors as Data Scientist, Data management expert, Data Security Engineers, Embedded Engineers, Mobile Application Developers, Gaming Technologist, Web Developers and software developers/quality Assurance/Testing. The department offers B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering which follows Choice Based Curriculum Systems (CBCS) with a domain focused curriculum that leads to produce technology expert and get awarded certification from industry. The department has collaboration with leading industries viz; AWS, IBM, Red Hat, UNITY and Dassault Systems in developing curriculum, course delivery and industry certification.

Welcome to MIDAS 2025

The 1st International Conference on “Machine Intelligence and Data Science (MIDAS-2025)” will be held from March 21 to 22, 2025, in Hybrid mode at School of Engineering and Technology, Centurion University of Technology and Management, Paralakhemundi, Odisha, India. MIDAS 2025 is the International Conference that aims at exploring growing advancements in the fields like application of machine intelligent systems in Data Management and Quality Analysis, Data Security and Privacy, Data Acquisition, Integration, Cleaning, Big Data Analysis, Data Mining, Data warehousing, and Data visualization. It is a technical congregation where the latest theoretical and technological techniques in advanced computing will be presented and discussed. The objective of the conference is to bring together experts from academic institutions, industries, research organizations and professional engineers for sharing of knowledge, expertise and experience in emerging trends related to machine intelligence in data science along with other related topics. All submissions will undergo blind peer review by three expert reviewers and the accepted papers will be presented during the conference. It has been planned to publish the accepted and presented papers as part of the conference proceedings with Springer in its Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series, subject to final approval from Springer.
